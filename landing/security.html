Our Commitment to Your Security and Privacy

Data Handling

At Speedgram, we prioritize the security and privacy of your data. Here's how we handle your information:

  • We do not store your Instagram login credentials on our servers.
  • Your session data is stored locally on your device and is encrypted.
  • We do not have access to your direct messages or personal Instagram data.
  • Any data transmitted between our app and Instagram's servers is done using secure, encrypted connections.

Security Practices

We employ several security measures to ensure the safety of your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for all communications.
  • Regular security audits and updates to our codebase.
  • Strict access controls and authentication measures.
  • Compliance with industry-standard security protocols.

Transparency

We believe in being transparent about our practices:

  • Our source code is open and available for review.
  • We provide clear information about how our app interacts with Instagram's API.
  • We promptly disclose any changes to our data handling or security practices.

Your Control

We empower you to maintain control over your data:

  • You can easily log out and clear all local data at any time.
  • We provide options to customize your privacy settings within the app.
  • You have the right to request deletion of any data associated with your account.
